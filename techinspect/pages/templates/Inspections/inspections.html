Vehicle Inspections

  • No play in the wheel bearings
  • Wheels are tight and have no visible crack
  • Hubcaps or center caps removed or firmly secured by lug nuts
  • Tires must be in good condition with no cords/belts showing or cracks
  • Tire tread depth (as appropriate)
  • Brake pads/rotors in good condition
  • No loose body panels
  • Numbers clearly visible on body
