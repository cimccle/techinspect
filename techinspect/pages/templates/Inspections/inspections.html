Vehicle Inspection

  • No play in the wheel bearings
  • Wheels are tight and have no visible crack
  • Hubcaps or center caps removed or firmly secured by lug nuts
  • Tires must be in good condition with no cords/belts showing or cracks
  • Tire tread depth (as appropriate)
  • Brake pads/rotors in good condition
  • No loose body panels
  • Numbers clearly visible on bod
  • Battery and connections secure
  • Air intake or box secure
  • Throttle cable secure (if applicable)
  • All Fluid caps secure
  • No major leaks apparent
  • Trunk is empty of items that move around
  • Functional exhaust system that's not excessivelt loud and exit behind driver
  • Numbers clearly visible on body
  • Floor mats and all other loose items removed from car
  • Brake, throttle, and clutch pedals secure
  • Firm brake pedal
  • No excessive play in steering
  • No excessive play in gear selector
  • Seat in fucntional order
  • Seat belt proper for year of car and class
  • Cameras phones and other gear mounted to the car
