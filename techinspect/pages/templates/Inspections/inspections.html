Vehicle Inspections

  • No play in the wheel bearings
  • Wheels are tight and have no visible cracks
  • Hub caps or center caps removed or firmly secured by lug nuts
  • Tires must be in good condition with no cord/belts showing or cracks
  • Tires tread depth
  • Break pads/rotars in good condition
  • No loose body panels
  • Numbers on & clearly visible on body
